RAFAEL BALDWIN

TV & MUSIC PRODUCER

Rafael Baldwin is a TV & music producer with 10 years of professional experience in both disciplines. As Producer and Director for broadcast and online, he has been responsible for coverage and special broadcasts of, among others, the BAFTA Awards, Cannes, Venice, Berlin, San Sebastian and London Film Festivals, The Royal Wedding in Britain and the Coronation of King Philip VI of Spain.

In the music field, he has composed and produced soundtracks that have been premiered at prestigious events such as the 2012 Cannes Film Festival ("Thirst of Life", Dir. Raquel Peralta), the 2013 Morelia Film Festival ("Heart of Gold" Dir. Jorge Ojeda), four premieres in 2011 and 2012 at The National Gallery in London.

In both disciplines he has also worked extensively as a film and music correspondent for important television and print outlets, such as Variety Latino, IndieRocks!, Univision Networks, Televisa Media Group, among others. He has interviewed reknowned figures, such as George Clooney, Tom Hanks, Reese Witherspoon, Nicole Kidman, Ethan Hawke, Viggo Mortensen, Michael Keaton, Jon Stewart, The Rolling Stones, The Eagles, Sandra Bullock, Nicholas Cage, Salma Hayek, among many others.

He is graduated in both fields from two of the leading institutions in Great Britain for broadcast and music; he received the MA in Screen Documentary – at Goldsmiths College, University of London – and the MMus in Composition for the Screen – from the Royal College of Music, which has among its alumni, acclaimed composers such as Andrew Lloyd Webber, Benjamin Britten, and Mark Anthony Turnage. He is currently undertaking the M.F.A. in Interactive Media at the University of Miami, where he is developing projects of interactive TV and music.
